# Lionsville

**Engineering the future.**

## Who we are

Lionsville is a technology company based in the Netherlands, founded by a small pride of innovators dedicated to reshaping the future through bold ideas. We believe technology only brings benefits when it removes a limitation — true experts in technology who connect to the real challenges our customers face know exactly the impact they make.

- 🦁 We are engineers at heart — pragmatic, hands-on, and outcome-driven
- ☁️ We build on the cloud — AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud
- 🤝 We work side by side with our customers on their real challenges
- 🚀 We ship enterprise-scale solutions that make a measurable difference

## What we do

We deliver enterprise-scale solutions across:

- **Cloud infrastructure & transformation** — migrations, landing zones, and cloud enablement
- **AI & intelligent automation** — from platforms to production
- **IoT & logistics systems** — think parcel automation handling millions of parcels a day
- **Data platforms & knowledge graphs** — turning data into connected insight
- **DevOps, observability & disaster recovery** — keeping the lights on, and knowing why they're on

Our toolbox includes Terraform, Kubernetes, Python, FastAPI, Node.js, Neo4j, GitHub, GitLab, Datadog, Grafana, and whatever else the job genuinely calls for.
